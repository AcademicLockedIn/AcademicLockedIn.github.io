About

Three languages, one direction.

I grew up navigating between cultures: a French education in Cambodia, a Khmer family, and English as the language I think in. That taught me early how to move between worlds and make them understand each other, whether in a Model UN committee in New York, a parliamentary office in Paris, or my family's restaurant in Phnom Penh.

In October 2025 I founded Voix Jeunesse, a civic initiative giving young people in the French-Cambodian community a platform to engage with public life. A few months later, I spent a month working in the office of Deputy Anne Genetet at the Assemblée nationale, contributing to work on French-Cambodian bilateral relations and the Francophonie 2026 summit.

My next step is a business education in the United States, with a long-term path through investment banking toward entrepreneurship. The thread connecting everything: putting communities, capital, and institutions in conversation.
